How to remove BLE from Xbox One S/X Controller

In this article

  1. Requirements
  2. Install the Xbox Accessories App
  3. Start the Legacy Flash Process

This topic describes how to restore the Legacy Firmware of your Xbox One S or X Bluetooth controller. Not all devices support the BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) version of the XINPUT protocol, especially older builds of Windows 10 or even 8.1/8/7. Here we gathered the information you will need to perform a downgrade.

Requirements

Downgrading with a PC:

  1. internet access
  2. Windows 10/11 x64 or later with latest updates
  3. Xbox Accessories app
  4. micro-USB (or Type-C for Xbox Series S/X Controllers) data cable

Downgrading with an Xbox:

  1. Xbox One or later
  2. internet access
  3. latest updates
  4. Xbox Accessories app
